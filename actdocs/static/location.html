Venue - CCBC Catonsville


View Larger Map

The venue this year is The Community College of Baltimore County, on the Catonsville campus. This campus is located south-west of Baltimore, right up the road from the BWI Airport.

Where to Stay

The DC Baltimore Perl Workshop organizers recommend the Comfort Suites at BWI Airport on Elkridge Landing Road for out of town attendees. They offer a free continental breakfast, 24 hour shuttle service, free parking and free transportation to and from the conference.

Comfort Suites at BWI is holding 25 rooms for us. Our exclusive hold on these rooms ends on March 23, 2012, so please book soon. The room rate is $94.00 plus tax per night. Please mention that this is for the "DC Baltimore Perl Workshop" event when reserving your room. The rate is only valid if you reserve your room before March 23, 2012 and mention the DCBPW event.

Call 410-691-1000 to reserve your room.
